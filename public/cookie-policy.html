Cookie Policy

NIL Matchup Platform

Last updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how you use our platform.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality
  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in securely
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Provide relevant content and advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your login session and security Until browser closes
Authentication Tokens Keep you logged in across sessions 30 days
Preference Cookies Remember your language and display preferences 1 year
Analytics Cookies Track platform usage and performance 2 years

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Stripe: Payment processing and security
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance
  • Supabase: Database and authentication services

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

Your Choices

When you first visit our platform, you can choose which types of cookies to accept. You can change these preferences at any time through your account settings.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our platform.
